This is the About page

Dungeon Quest has many different mechanics, while in the dungeon you have a chance for a boss named "Gregg" to spawn in and if you defeat him you get rewarded with extra loot and random amount of materials ranging from 1-3. You can use these materials in the lobby to craft new weapons which are stronger than the weapons you drop from the dungeon. As you progress through all the dungeons they get exponentially more difficult to complete giving the player more of a challenge, You can also trade other players to get loot for your current level. Their are also cosmetics that you can grind for by doing quests, You can get cosmetic Armors, Weapons, Enchants, Auras and Titles.
