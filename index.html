This is the Home page

Dungeon Quest is a Roblox RPG game. It has a multitude of dungeons for the player to work through and defeat, once you have finished the dungeon you are rewarded with loot you can upgrade, sell or equip to get stronger. you have 3 main classes which are Mage, Warrior and Guardian. You have access to different equipment per level you aquire, the equipment you can get are Helmets, Chestplate, Weapons, Rings and Spells. Dungeon Quest also has multiple character slots so if you reach the highest dungeon you can create another character and play from the start all over again.
